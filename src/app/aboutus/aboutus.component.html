Welcome to Ristorante Il Gambero Rosso

About Us

Welcome to Ristorante Il Gambero Rosso, where authentic Italian cuisine meets a warm and welcoming atmosphere. Situated in the heart of Padova Province, our restaurant is a culinary haven where the traditions of Italy come to life, offering a truly immersive experience for all your senses.


Our Story

At Ristorante Il Gambero Rosso, our story begins with a deep love for the rich and diverse flavors of Italy. Our founders, Luca Rossi, Alessia Martini and Marco Moretti, brought their passion for Italian gastronomy to Padova Province with the dream of sharing their family's time-honored recipes with the world. The result is a restaurant that captures the essence of Italy through its cuisine, setting, and hospitality.


A Family Tradition

At Ristorante Il Gambero Rosso, we treat every guest like family. Our restaurant is a place where laughter and conversation flow as freely as our wine, and where every bite transports you to the streets of Rome, the hills of Tuscany, or the shores of Sicily. We invite you to become a part of our extended family and share in our love for all things Italian.
